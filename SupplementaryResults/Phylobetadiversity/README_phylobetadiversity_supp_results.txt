Choosing the number of clusters (PRs) for each component of the phylobetadiversity of lizard, marsupial and rodent communities based on the explained dissimilarity and silhouette. The best number of clusters obtained for each resolution is indicated by the circle with a cross. For more details, see the main text and Supplementary Material.

This folder contains the complementary results of the phylobetadiversity analyzes inferred by the scripts available in https://github.com/chouerik/PE_manuscript/tree/main/phylospatial_scripts/06_phylobetadiversity
